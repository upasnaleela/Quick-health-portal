Welcome to Quick Health Portal

In this modern world everything can be controlled and accessed without the presence of a particular person - that which the time constraint of the modern word demands. This concept is implemented in a complete way through the QUICK HEALTH PORTAL is project. It is not practical in today's life to spend much time only being dedicated for a particular work. The admin module is concerned with updating number of patients and doctors in the medicine portal. The admin can add doctors to portal and enter their detail like Name, Qualification and Specialization etc. He can view the detail of both patients and doctors and has right to delete from medicine portal if necessary and also update the news. And patient only need to some type of medicine name based on comparison and side effect Company wise and get nearest location of medical store easily using QUICK HEALTH PORAL. There is also an option for both the patient and the doctor to do live chatting.

Key Features

Key features in the system are

  • Search medicine effectively
  • Comparison and side effects of medicine
  • Chatting with doctor
  • Access news
  • Medical store information
